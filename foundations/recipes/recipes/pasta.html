Creamy Garlic Pasta

Description

This creamy garlic pasta is rich, comforting, and perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Saut√© garlic in butter until fragrant.
  3. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add pasta, season, and mix well.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
