Fresh Garden Salad

Description

This fresh garden salad is light, healthy, and packed with crisp vegetables. Perfect as a side dish or a light meal on its own.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups lettuce leaves
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables.
  2. Chop lettuce, halve cherry tomatoes, slice cucumber, and grate carrot.
  3. Combine all vegetables in a large bowl.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Toss gently and serve fresh.
